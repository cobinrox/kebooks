Scalloped Potatoes with Ham, cont.

SCALLOPED POTATOES WITH HAM INSTRUCTIONS
 1. Spray the bowl (pan) with non-stick cooking spray.
 2. To the bowl, add a layer of potatoes.
 3. On top of the first layer of potatoes, add a layer of ham.
 4. Add another layer of potatoes.
 5. Add another layer of ham.
 6. Continue to layer, leaving about 1 inch or so from the top of the bowl or pan.
 7. Pour cream on top of the top layer until it covers the top layer.(Don't add so much that it will bubble over when cooked.)
 8. Cook at 350 for 1-1½ hours.
 9. Broil for a few minutes to brown the outer layer if needed.


Scalloped, NOT Scalped !
