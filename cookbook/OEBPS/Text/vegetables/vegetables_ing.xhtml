EASY VEGETABLES INGREDIENTS (serves approx. 2)
Note: frozen vegetables often do not appear in real life as they do on the package. This is especially true of bags of "mixed" vegetables and bags of brocolli, the latter often missing and instead replaced with brocolli "sticks". In Gramma Vi's recipe, she recommends individual frozen packages for each type of vegetable, except for peas and carrots. If you wind up with a bag of brocolli sticks, be sure to return it to the store: Let them know that you want quality products.
 1 c Frozen lima beans
 1 c Frozen Italian green beans
 1 c Frozen soy beans
 1 c Frozen brocolli
 1 c Frozen peas and carrots
 1/2 tbs Oil and vinegar salad dressing (Gramma Vi's favorite is Newman's Own® brand)
 1/4 tsp salt
 1-2 tsp pepper 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
TEMPLATE TOOLS/EQUIPMENT
 1 microwave steamer bowl (similar to the Norpro®)
 Microwave oven


Microwave steamer bowl with removable insert.
