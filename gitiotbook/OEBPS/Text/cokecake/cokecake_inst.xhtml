To-Die-For Coke® Cake, cont.

COKE CAKE INSTRUCTIONS -- CAKE
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
 1. Spray cake pan with cooking spray.
 2. In bowl 1, combine (optionally sift) sugar (2 c) and flour (2 c)
 3. Add marshmallows (1 ½ c).
 4. In a pan, mix butter (1 c or ½ c), oil (½ c), cocoa (¾ c) and Coke (1 c). Bring to a boil and power over contents in bowl 1, mix well.
 5. In bowl 2, mix baking soda (1 tsp) with buttermilk (½ c). Add this to bowl 1 mixture.
 6. Add eggs (2) and vanilla (1 tsp) to bowl 1's mixture.
 7. Lick spoon.
 8. If there is any batter left after licking spoon, pour into cake pan, bake 35-45 minutes.
 9. While cake is baking, prepare frosting.
 8. Frost immediately.
COKE CAKE INSTRUCTIONS -- FROSTING !
 1. In the saucepan over medium-high heat, mix together the butter (½ c), cocoa (¼ c) and Coke (½ c).
 2. Stir constantly until the butter melts or mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat.
 3. Add confectioner's sugar (4 c) slowly to the frosting mixture and mix thoroughly.
 4. Lick spoon.
 4. If there is any frosting left after licking the spoon, frost the cake as soon as it is removed from oven.
