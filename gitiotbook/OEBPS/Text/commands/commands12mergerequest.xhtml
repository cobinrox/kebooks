Making a Merge/Pull Request

A merge or pull request is a feature of Git managment servers that is used to message team members to let them know that your branch has been finished and pushed up to the remote site and is ready to be officially merged with a parent branch on the remote. Your team may or may not use these requests, but just be advised that some teams may require this.
