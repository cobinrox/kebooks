DIVINITY INSTRUCTIONS
 1. In bowl 1, combine the cereal (1 1/2c), oil (1/4c), water (1/2c).
 2. In bowl 2, combine egg, buttermilk (1c), molasses (1/8c), then add to the cereal mix.
 3. Reusing bowl 2, combine flour (1 1/8c), sugar (2 tsp), baking soda (1 1/4 tsp) and salt (1/4 tsp), then add to cereal mix.
 4. Cover mixture with plastic wrap or towel and let stand for 15m to 1 hr (the longer the better).
 5. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
 6. Spray muffin tin/w cooking spray.
 7. Fill each muffin cup 3/4 full.
 8. Cook until spongy.
