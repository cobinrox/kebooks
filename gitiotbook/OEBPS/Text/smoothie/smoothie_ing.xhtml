Easy Smoothie, cont.

Easy SMOOTHIE INGREDIENTS (serves approx. 1-2)
1 c Frozen Cherries
1 c Frozen Wild Blueberries (wild blueberries are sweeter than tame blueberries)
1 scoop Vanilla Protein Powder (Vegan powder is also available, or skip powder if you do not like milk-shake-like flavour and/or texture)
3 tbs Ground Flax Seed
1 c Water
TOOLS/EQUIPMENT
Ninja (or equivalent) mixer
