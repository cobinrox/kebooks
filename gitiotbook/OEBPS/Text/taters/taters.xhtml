Scalloped Potatoes
SUMMARY
This is a Gramma Vi staple: Scalloped Potatoes¹ with Ham. Scalloped implies a casserole or layering technique, and Gramma Vi's scalloped potatoes use cream and ham for the layering effect. Use the broiler (see instructions) to give them a nice browning on top.
¹Potatoes is the plural form of potato, the latter sometimes confused with potatoe.
