Scalloped Potatoes with Ham, cont.

Gramma Vi makes her scalloped potatoes on-the-fly, without an exact amount of ingredients. So, with that in mind, here are the ingredients, though the precise amount will vary based on the size of your baking bowl (or pan).
SCALLOPED POTATOES INGREDIENTS
  non-stick cooking spray
  Thinly-peeled potatoes (number will vary on the size of your baking container)
  1 Ham steak (or ad-hoc ham), diced up
  Whipping cream
Scalloped Potatoes TOOLS/EQUIPMENT
  100-year ode (old) baking bowl (or substitue with a baking pan)
