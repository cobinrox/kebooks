Eggless Egg Salad, cont.



EGGLESS EGG SALAD INGREDIENTS
  12 ounces extra-firm tofu, can be drained over night  ½ tsp tumeric
 ¼ tsp mild curry powder  ¼ tsp garlic powder
  1 tbs low-sodium soy sauce  ¼ tsp onion powder
  1 whole celery stalk, minced  ½ tsp black salt
  1 ¼ tbs nutritional yeast  1 ½ tbs Dijon mustard
  2 tbs relish  2 tbs fat-free vegan mayo
EGGLESS EGG SALAD TOOLS/EQUIPMENT
  Optional for draining tofu: sieve
  Vegetable chopper or scary-sharp knife
  Bowl
