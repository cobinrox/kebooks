Bran Muffins, cont.

BRAN MUFFIN INGREDIENTS
  non-stick cooking spray (or paper muffin cups)
  1 1/2 c All-Bran® (or other shredded bran cereal)
  1/4 c Vegetable oil
  1/2 c Boiling water
  1 egg
  1 c buttermilk
  1/8 c molasses
  1 1/8 c whole-wheat flour
  2 tsp sugar
  1 1/4 tsp baking soda
  1/4 tsp salt
BRAN MUFFIN TOOLS/EQUIPMENT
  2 bowls
  plastic wrap or damp towel
  muffin tin
