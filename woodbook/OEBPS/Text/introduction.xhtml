Introduction -- from the editor

Heads Like These!


Heads !

Heads? Heads? Heads is a general term that is used to describe a population or group. For example, when driving down an interstate and a mini-van is in front of you, what do you see? Heads. Or, when you are hiking on a popular steep hiking trail, and stop to take a look to see who all is behind you, what do you see? More heads. Finally, when it is a Saturday afternoon and inside the store you turn a corner to approach a check-out lane, what are you confronted by? Heads, of course.

This book provides recipes that heads like! They have been tested on many heads and have been proven to be well-received. So when Gramma Vi says "Heads Like This!", you can be assured that the resulting concoction will be consumed like a mad heard of zombies chowing down a pit of rotting brains.

Unique Ingredients

These recipes are well-received by heads, and you may find new, unusual, or unexpected ingredients in some of them. Take the What-Causes-That-Crunch? Easy Potato Salad, the Oh-That's-How-You-Make-it-So-Good Pie Crust, or the beloved Coke® Cake-- they all have some interesting embellishments.

Experiment and Substitute

While heads like these recipes (and heads like food), be advised that not all heads will snarf down all recipes with the same zeal. Flavour, taste, and all-around yumminess is subjective: Therefore some recipes will appeal to certain heads, while others may not appeal as much. Onions, for example, are a polarizing subject in some circles of heads and could therefore be left out of otherwise perfectly-acceptable dishes.


Be sensitive to potent foods.

The dark side is also sought out by a small but discerning band of heads; no, not dark as in dark chocolate, but dark as in BURNED. Like burned cookies, burned toast, and burned crust. So feel free to substitute cooking times, leave as-is, or burn as needed.

And don't forget the vegans: some recipes can be easily converted to a vegan's approval.


Vegans !

As for nutritional value, be wary of some of the desserts or meals that involve cream-- substitute as you deem necessary.

Bang-for-the-Buck

Some of the recipes are time-consuming. But, the bang-for-the-buck ratio is usually a high number. For those recipes that take longer time than is usual, a bang-for-the-buck icon is provided to remind you that you will be pleased with the reception of the final product.
