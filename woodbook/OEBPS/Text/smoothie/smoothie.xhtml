Easy Smoothie

       
       
 

Simplest Smoothie.
SUMMARY
There are a lot of smoothies and a lot of ingredients that can be added. This one is simple and that is part of its appeal. The main recipe calls for a scoop of vanilla protein powder, but some heads prefer their smoothies without the powder, which otherwise gives the smoothie a milkshake-like flavour. There are also vegan protein powders that can be substituted.
