Eggless Egg Salad
SUMMARY
Vegans live a tough life. After all, they can only eat Oreos. This eggless egg salad, or vegg salad is a perfectly noble meal for vegans and non-vegans alike. It also has a lot of fun ingredients, but feel free to substitute or leave absent some if they are not to your taste.
