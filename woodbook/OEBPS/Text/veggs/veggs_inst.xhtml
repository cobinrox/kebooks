Eggless Egg Salad, cont.

EGGLESS EGG SALAD INSTRUCTIONS
 1. If desired, or if you are unable to obtain extra-firm tofu, place the tofu atop a paper towel seated in a sieve over a bowl in the refrigerator. Let drain overnight.
 2. With vegetable chopper, or sharp instrument or even your hands, chop up or crumble up tofu into small chunks.
 3. Add tofu to bowl.
 4. Add all the rest of the ingredients and stir, stir, stir.
